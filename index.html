Introduction

Crafty is a website that offers a variety of instructions and video suggestions for making handcrafted items. People may learn how to make handcrafted products here at all levels, from beginner to advanced.

This project was created in order to complete one of the BTEC tasks. We were instructed to include a Home Page, About Us, Contact Us, and Courses sections. The exhibited courses must contain at least three training courses, and the tutorials for each training must have its own unique information.
